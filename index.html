SOCIETY FOR WELFARE OF THE HANDICAPPED (Regd.)

It is a very proud, and momentous day for Society for Welfare of The Handicapped, managing Patiala School for The Deaf, Patiala School for The Blind and Patiala School for The DeafBlind that our Society has been adjudged as the BEST NGO in India in Child welfare activities. This has been possible due to the unrelenting support from the Managing Committee, staff and students that this honour has been bestowed.

adjudged as the BEST NGO in India

The President of India presented the award in Rashtrapati Bhawan, New Delhi in person to Col Karaminder Singh on 14 Nov 2015 at 1400 hrs. In 2014 we were adjudged as the best NGO of Punjab State.

About

We make the children - Service providers - not service seekers

Society for Welfare of The Handicapped Regd. is a pioneer in North India in the field of education for Children with special needs. Society was established on 16 Apr 1967.

Right from the first day to date, No fees are charged from the students and everything still continues to be free of cost to...

Education is provided in a scientific manner by trained staff. Proper audiometry tests are periodically done to test the hearing capabilities and change our pattern of teaching accordingly. The school has a special speech therapy center which is managed by trained staff. After class XII the students are provided vocational training in the trade of their choice to enable them to work on their own and not be dependent on any one. For the deaf children we also have a marriage bureau service for appropriate match making.
